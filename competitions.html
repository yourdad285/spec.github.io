Competitions For ECE Students

Texas Instruments Innovation Challenge

It is organized by Texas Instruments India every year for engineering students. This is a very good competition for DIY enthusiast and a very good opportunity to learn electronics hands-on. You have to work in teams (2-5 members). If your proposal is accepted you can order ICs /EVMs upto $200 from Texas Instruments besides the free samples that get from (http://www.ti.com) . For more details check out the website (Innovation Challenge: India).

The Freescale Cup

The Freescale Cup is a global competition where student teams build, program, and race a model car around a track for speed. The fastest car to complete the track without derailing, wins. So, it requires participants to have good electronics and mechanical engineering skills. For more details check out the website (India | Freescale Community).

Cadence design Contest

Cadence Design Contest is a flagship initiative of Cadence University Program. This program is open to undergraduate and graduate students. For more details check out the website. (Cadence Design Contest 2015).
